About Me

I was born in London and educated here my whole life. From a young age I have always taken an interest in electronics and every thing related to it from computers to hardware. At 16, I brought various computer components online and built one up myself, granted it's not too difficult these days if you know what you're doing with all the resources out there. I really took an interest in software development and programming in my first year of university when I was exposed to the hardware embedded level of C programming on TI's MSP430 Launchpad. I excelled at tackling problems related to this and enjoyed finding new and innovative ways of doing things.

I completed my A Levels in Mathematics, Physics and Economics at St Paul's Way Trust School in June 2015 achieving A*A*A. I was then accepted in to University College London to study BEng Electrical and Electronic Engineering between 2015 to 2018 where I have achieved a first class honours in my first and second year.

My goal is to help people strive towards a more digital and modern world. My education in engineering along with experience at various institutions has provided me with a fantastic foundation to build upon.
